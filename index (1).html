A Farewell, Written With Gratitude

Dear NBSC,

As I prepare to conclude my academic journey at Northern Bukidnon State College, I find myself reflecting on the years that have shaped not only my education but also my character. What once began as an unfamiliar environment gradually became a place of learning, growth, and countless unforgettable experiences.

Throughout my time here, NBSC provided more than academic instruction. It became a foundation where discipline was developed, perspectives were broadened, and meaningful relationships were formed. Every challenge, every success, and every setback contributed to the person I am today. These experiences will remain valuable long after I leave these halls.

Graduation is often viewed as the completion of a goal. However, I see it as the beginning of another pursuit. While new opportunities now await beyond the campus, I recognize that this transition is not merely a matter of choice but part of the path that destiny unfolds for each of us. There is still much to learn, many places to explore, and many aspirations yet to be realized. As the familiar words of a timeless song suggest, I still haven't found what I'm looking for. To me, those words are not an expression of emptiness but of purpose — a reminder that growth continues beyond every milestone.

I extend my sincere appreciation to the faculty, staff, and administrators for their commitment to nurturing generations of students. Your guidance has left a lasting impact that reaches far beyond the classroom. To my classmates and friends, thank you for making this journey memorable through shared experiences, collaboration, and unwavering support.

Although I now move toward new horizons, I will always carry the values and lessons I gained from NBSC. This institution will remain an important part of my story — not as the final destination, but as the place where my pursuit of knowledge, purpose, and personal growth was strengthened.

Thank you, NBSC, for being the foundation upon which I will continue building my future. While my journey leads me elsewhere, this chapter will always hold a special place in my life.

Respectfully yours,

Jam Piodos
